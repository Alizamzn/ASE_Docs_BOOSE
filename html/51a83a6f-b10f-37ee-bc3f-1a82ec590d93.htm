      MyInt Operators and Type Conversions

      Operators

      Addition(MyInt, MyInt) Adds two MyInt values.
      Division(MyInt, MyInt) Divides one MyInt value by another.
      (Int32 to MyInt) Implicitly converts an Int32 to a MyInt.
      (MyInt to Int32) Implicitly converts a MyInt to an Int32.
      Multiply(MyInt, MyInt) Multiplies two MyInt values.
      Subtraction(MyInt, MyInt) Subtracts one MyInt value from another.
